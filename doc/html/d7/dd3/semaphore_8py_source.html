7 A primitive semaphore is one of the oldest synchronization primitives in the

9 Edsger W. Dijkstra (he used `P()` and `V()` instead of `acquire()` and
10 `release()`).
11 
12 A semaphore manages an internal counter which is decremented by each
13 `acquire()` call and incremented by each `release()` call. The counter can
14 never go below zero; when `acquire()` finds that it is zero, it blocks,
